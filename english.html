Form of active and passive voice in tenses.



what is PAST PARTICIPLE?


simple present tense
active passive
sub. + verb + -s/-es/-ies
sub. + am/is/are + past participle + by + obj.
Ali likes the cat.
The cat is cared by Ali.



simple present contunues tense
active passive
sub. + am/is/are + present participle + obj.
sub. + am/is/are + being + past participle + by + obj.
She is writting a novel.
A novel is being written by her.



simple present perfect tense
active passive
sub. + has/have + past participle + obj.
sub. + has/have + been + past participle + by + obj.
Ali has paid the water bill.
The water bill has been paid by him.



simple present perfect countinuous tense
active passive
sub. + has/have + been + past participle + obj.
sub. + has/have + been + being + past participle + by + obj.
For all these years, Ali has been paying the water bill.
For all these years, the water bill has been being paid by Ali.



simple past tense
active passive
sub. + verb + -ed
sub. + was/were + past participle + by + obj.
Ali cleaned the table.
The table was cleaned by Ali.



simple past continuous tense
active passive
sub. + was/were + present participle + obj.
sub. + was/were + being + past participle + by + obj.
I was waiting for Vincent.
Vincent was being waited by me.



simple past perfect tense
active passive
sub. + had + past participle + obj.
sub. + had + been + past participle + by + obj.
He had left the company.
The company had been left by me.



simple past perfect tense
active passive
sub. + had + been + present participle + object
sub. + had + been + being + past participle + by + obj.
Jalil had been teaching English for ten years.
English had been being taught by Jalil for ten years.



simple future continuous tense
active passive
sub. + shall/will + be + present participle + obj.
sub. + will/shall + be + being + past participle + by + object
I shall be doing a test tomorrow.
A test will be being done by me tomorrow.



simple future perfect tense
active passive
sub. + will/shall + have + past participle + obj.
sub. + will/shall + have + been + past participle + by + obj.
I shall have finished the job by Monday.
The job will have been finished (by me) by Monday.

sub. + am/is/are + going to + have + past participle + obj.
sub. + am/is/are + going to + have + been + past participle + by + obj.
They are going to have completed the task before noon.
The task is going to have been completed before noon.



simple future perfect countinuous tense
active passive
sub. + will/shall + have + been + present participle
sub. + will + have + been + being + past participle + by + obj.
In November, Ali was have been paying the water bill for three years.
In November, the water bill will have been being paid by Ali for three years.
